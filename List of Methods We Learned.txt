* METHODS OF STRING CLASS:
.charAt(), .indexOf(), .lastIndexOf(), .substring(), .replace(), .split() .equals(), .equalsIgnoreCase, .toLowerCase(), .toUpperCase(),

Remember: You can use a 'for' loop and 'charAt(i)' to traverse on all the letters of a string.

Note: While using the 'replace()' method; be aware that we have the ability to traverse over all the characters of a string, to find the target character we're searching. Ex:
str.replace(targetChar, newChar)
